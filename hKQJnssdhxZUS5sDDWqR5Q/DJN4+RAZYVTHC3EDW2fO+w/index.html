Anaerobic strength and power / vertical jump

 
Vertical jump test

The vertical jump test is a standard countermovement jump without a run-up. Values are entered in cm and can be entered as decimal values [such as 43.5 cm]. Sporting norms are based on reported values from a range of published scientific reports and other references such as textbooks in sport and exercise science. However, some sports have very few datapoints and this means there is less reliability for these sports. If you hav know of elite-level normative data then please contact the author of this program - Professor Kevin Norton - to advise him of the source of the normative data or send them to his email address [k.norton@unisa.edu.au].
